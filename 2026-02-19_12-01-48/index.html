Ingredients
0.5 cl - benedictine
1.5 cl - campari
1.5 cl - gin
4 cl - scotch
Serve in

Coupe glass

Preparation

1. Pour benedictine, campari, gin and scotch into a mixing glass.

2. Add ice and stir with a bar spoon for 15s.

3. Strain into the glass.
